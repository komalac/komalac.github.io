Tumor treatment Analysis


Pymaceuticals Inc., is based out of San Diego, CA. Pymaceuticals specializes in drug-based, anti-cancer pharmaceuticals. In our most recent efforts, we begun screening for potential treatments to squamous cell carcinoma (SCC).

Purpose of this project was to analyze how tumor responds to each drug in study. Two datasets, 'Clinical trail' and 'Drug data' are used in this analysis. This dataset records the observation of treatment given to 250 mouse with 10 different drugs.
